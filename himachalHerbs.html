From the Heart of Himachal to Your Skin.

Apple Cinnamon Preserve

Apples also known as ‘kushu’ in Ladakh have more than 11 local varieties which are small and juicy. Apples are a good source of antioxidants, fiber, water, and several nutrients and capability to tolerate moisture.

Apricot Jam

Apricot, locally known as ‘chuli’ having a unique taste. It is classified into two broad categories based on kernel taste, Khante and Narmo. ‘Raktsey Karpo’, apricots with white seed stone, is unique to Ladakh.

Apricot Cinnamon Jam

More properly known as “Chuli” or “Lal Khubani” or “jardalu”, these apricots from Ladakh are the most common fruit of the Ladakh province in Northern India.

Apricot Ginger Jam

Apricot, locally known as ‘chuli’ having a unique taste.It is known for sweetness, juiciness, aroma, flesh color and increases metabolism, improves digestion. It is rich in vitamin A, C, E and Iron, copper, potassium etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

HH is a sustainable brand under the Himalayan Institute of Alternatives (HIAL) that promotes local and organic products. Our products are crafted by students and local artisans from the pristine region of Himachal, using naturally sourced ingredients.

What kind of products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of organic food items and natural beauty products. All our offerings are made using eco-friendly processes and are free from harmful chemicals or artificial additives.

Are all HH products organic?

Yes, we ensure that all of our products are 100% organic and naturally sourced. Our aim is to provide you with the purest products while preserving the environment and supporting local communities.

How does HH support students and local artisans?

HH provides hands-on experience for students in product development, marketing, and sales. We also work with local artisans, helping them showcase their skills and earn fair wages by crafting these unique products.

Where are HH products made?

All HH products are produced locally in Himachal. Our teams work closely with the natural resources of the region to bring you high-quality, eco-friendly products that reflect the purity of Himalaya.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website. Simply browse through our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We offer secure payment options and reliable delivery services.

Do you ship products outside of Himachal?

Yes, we ship HH products nationwide across India. Currently, we are working on expanding our delivery services internationally as well.

Hands behind Himachal Herbs

About Priya

Meet the visionary behind Himachal Herbs, a young woman from the serene hills of Himachal Pradesh, whose love for nature and traditional remedies led her to create a brand that celebrates the power of the earth. Her journey began with a deep respect for the wisdom of her ancestors, who cherished the healing properties of the region’s natural treasures. Driven by this passion, she brings together pure ingredients like apricot and medicinal herbs, transforming them into products that are both nourishing and sustainable. With each product she crafts, she aims to preserve her heritage while promoting a modern, eco-friendly lifestyle.
