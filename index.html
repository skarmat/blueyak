From the Roots of Ladakh, for the World

Apple Cinnamon Preserve

Apples also known as ‘kushu’ in Ladakh have more than 11 local varieties which are small and juicy. Apples are a good source of antioxidants, fiber, water, and several nutrients and capability to tolerate moisture.

Apricot Jam

Apricot, locally known as ‘chuli’ having a unique taste. It is classified into two broad categories based on kernel taste, Khante and Narmo. ‘Raktsey Karpo’, apricots with white seed stone, is unique to Ladakh.

Apricot Cinnamon Jam

More properly known as “Chuli” or “Lal Khubani” or “jardalu”, these apricots from Ladakh are the most common fruit of the Ladakh province in Northern India.

Apricot Ginger Jam

Frequently Asked Questions

Blue Yak is a sustainable brand under the Himalayan Institute of Alternatives (HIAL) that promotes local and organic products. Our products are crafted by students and local artisans from the pristine region of Ladakh, using naturally sourced ingredients.

What kind of products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of organic food items and natural beauty products. All our offerings are made using eco-friendly processes and are free from harmful chemicals or artificial additives.

Are all Blue Yak products organic?

Yes, we ensure that all of our products are 100% organic and naturally sourced. Our aim is to provide you with the purest products while preserving the environment and supporting local communities.

How does Blue Yak support students and local artisans?

Blue Yak provides hands-on experience for students in product development, marketing, and sales. We also work with local artisans, helping them showcase their skills and earn fair wages by crafting these unique products.

Where are Blue Yak products made?

All Blue Yak products are produced locally in Ladakh. Our teams work closely with the natural resources of the region to bring you high-quality, eco-friendly products that reflect the purity of Ladakh.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website. Simply browse through our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We offer secure payment options and reliable delivery services.

Do you ship products outside of Ladakh?

Yes, we ship Blue Yak products nationwide across India. Currently, we are working on expanding our delivery services internationally as well.
